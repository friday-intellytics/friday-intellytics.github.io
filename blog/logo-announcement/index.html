Fast Insights, Meet Grad

Grad is the mark behind Friday's AI-Assisted Analytics: an Analytics Experience built to turn the strongest signal into Fast Insights.

The Analytics Experience behind the mark

In math, grad (∇) points in the direction of steepest increase. That idea fits AI-Assisted Analytics: move toward the strongest signal first.

In machine learning, you apply ∇ to error, follow the gradient, and adjust until the system gets better. The symbol is directional, rigorous, and a little nerdy.

That is also how we think about the Analytics Experience. Start with a business question, use AI-Assisted Analytics to narrow the search, and hand people Fast Insights they can act on.

So we made grad our mark. The interactive sidebar lets you preview a few wordmark directions and icon treatments, but the underlying idea stays the same: serious systems should feel clear, fast, and confident.
